ℹ️ About this site

This site exists for one thing: following the Grand Slams. It is an independent, non-commercial fan project — dates, results, champions and rankings for the four tennis majors, nothing more. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by or connected to the ATP, WTA, ITF, the Australian Open, Roland-Garros, Wimbledon, the US Open, ESPN or YouTube. Tournament and player names are used only to refer to the events and results they describe; all trademarks belong to their owners.

Scores, schedules and rankings are factual sports results, read from ESPN's public feed. Nothing is hosted or re-uploaded here: ▶ Highlights links go to the tournaments' own official YouTube channels, and player photos load straight from ESPN's public image server.

Win percentages shown on upcoming matches are market prices from Polymarket, fetched server-side and shown for information only — they are predictions by market participants, not facts, not endorsements and not betting advice. This site is not affiliated with Polymarket.

Privacy: no cookies, no analytics, no ads, no accounts, no tracking of any kind. Your ⭐ follows and timezone choice are stored only in your own browser and never leave it. The only external requests your browser makes are for player photos (ESPN), highlight thumbnails (YouTube; a video itself loads — via YouTube's cookie-less embed domain — only when you tap play), and one anonymous ping to a public visit counter, which carries no cookies and no identifiers — it just adds 1.

If you represent a rights holder and have a concern about anything shown here, please open an issue on the GitHub repository — it will be addressed promptly.
